NEXUS MIND is a collection of 256 AI trading agent identity NFTs formed through HACD Stack.

## How Stack works

HACD Stack is how new assets are formed on the Hacash blockchain.

When you Stack a NEXUS MIND:
1. You provide one HACD diamond
2. You pay the stack cost in HAC (+ network fee)
3. One NEXUS MIND NFT is formed on that diamond — permanently, on-chain
4. The HACD's inscription becomes the agent's permanent on-chain name

If the stack is ever removed, the NEXUS MIND is burned. The identity is gone. This is what makes each formation permanent and meaningful.

Yes. HAC is required for the stack cost and network fee at formation time.

Yes, up to 4 per wallet in the public phase.
