# NEXUS MIND — Issuer FAQ

## Do participants need HACD?
Yes. Each NEXUS MIND formation requires exactly one HACD diamond per NFT. There is no way to form a NEXUS MIND without providing HACD.

## Do participants need HAC?
Yes. HAC is required for two things: the stack cost (paid to the HACD Stack protocol) and the Hacash network fee. Both must be available in the participant's wallet before stacking.

## How many NEXUS MIND can one wallet form?
Up to 4 in the public phase. This limit is set to prevent single-wallet dominance and preserve the distributed nature of the collection. Subject to Launchpad enforcement.

## What is the total supply?
256 NEXUS MIND NFTs. Hard-capped. No additional supply can be issued after the 256 lots are formed.

## What determines a NEXUS MIND agent's archetype?
The HACD inscription (the unique 6-letter name of the diamond) maps to one of four archetype classes: Momentum, Contrarian, Arbitrageur, or Sentinel. The mapping is determined before launch and published publicly.

## What happens if someone removes their stack?
The NEXUS MIND NFT is burned and permanently destroyed. The HACD diamond is released. This reinforces the permanence of each active formation.

## Is there a designated address phase?
No. All 256 lots are open to public participation simultaneously. There is no reserved or pre-allocated phase.

## Is NEXUS MIND an investment product?
No. NEXUS MIND is a HACD Stack Asset — an AI agent identity NFT formed on-chain. No financial return, price floor, liquidity, or listing is guaranteed. Participants form an agent identity, not an investment position.

## What utility exists at launch?
At launch, each NEXUS MIND holder has a permanent on-chain AI agent identity with a PoW formation record and an archetype classification. Integration with the CycleMind AI trading platform is planned but not live at formation time.

## Where can participants verify their formed NEXUS MIND?
On explorer.hacash.org and on the HACD Launchpad page after formation is confirmed on-chain.

## Who built NEXUS MIND?
NEXUS MIND is built by an independent AI builder with live AI trading platforms: CycleMind (cyclemind.vercel.app) and BitEdge (bitedge.vercel.app). Active on X (@cyclemindarc / @defitony0x) and Telegram (@defitony0x).
